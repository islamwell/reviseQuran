How to Teach Gently

1Recite the ayah slowly, then ask your listener to repeat it back to you — listening to yourself correct them is deep revision.
2Explain the meaning in your own simple words. If you stumble, that's a weak spot — the app will schedule it.
3One ayah a day is enough. Patience with others is patience with yourself.
